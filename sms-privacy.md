# Strang, Inc. SMS Privacy Notice

Strang, Inc. uses SMS text messaging solely for professional, business‑related communication with clients, contractors, consultants, and project stakeholders. Messages may include scheduling coordination, project updates, meeting confirmations, and responses to inquiries related to active pursuits or projects.

Mobile numbers are collected directly from individuals as part of an existing professional relationship and are used only for the intended business purpose. Strang, Inc. does not sell, rent, or share mobile numbers for marketing or promotional purposes.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Individuals may opt out at any time by replying **STOP**.
